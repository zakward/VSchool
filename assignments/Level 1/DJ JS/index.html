LOOK AT MY COOL BOX

    The Square's COLOR will change as follows:
  • BLUE when the mouse hovers over the square or "B" is typed on keyboard
  • RED when the mouse button is held down over the square or "R" typed on keyboard
  • YELLOW when the mouse button is let go over the square or "Y" typed on keyboard
  • GREEN when the mouse is double clicked in the square or "G" is typed on keyboard
  • ORANGE when the mouse scroll is used in the window or "O" is typed on keyboard
